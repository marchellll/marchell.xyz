Another Bible (“the App”) is developed and operated by Marchell Imanuel (“we”, “us”, or “our”).

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

⸻

## 1. Overview

Another Bible is a Bible reading application designed to provide a clean and distraction-free reading experience.

The App can be used without creating an account.

We respect your privacy. We do not sell, collect, or share personal data.

⸻

## 2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Specifically, the App does not collect:
	•	Name
	•	Email address
	•	Phone number
	•	Physical address
	•	Device identifiers (including IDFA or Advertising ID)
	•	IP address
	•	Location data
	•	Usage analytics
	•	Crash reports
	•	Tracking data

The App does not use cookies, tracking SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising frameworks.

⸻

## 3. Account Registration

The App does not require or support account registration.

No login functionality is provided.

⸻

## 4. Data Storage

All user-generated content (such as bookmarks, highlights, or notes, if available) is stored locally on the user’s device.

No data is transmitted to any server operated by us.

⸻

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate:
	•	Analytics services
	•	Advertising networks
	•	Cloud storage services
	•	Social login providers

No third-party SDKs are used for data collection.

⸻

## 6. Content Rights

The App currently distributes:
	•	AYT (Alkitab Yang Terbuka), under its open license
	•	KJV (King James Version), which is in the public domain

Users may import their own Bible texts into the App. Imported content:
	•	Remains on the user’s device
	•	Is not uploaded
	•	Is not accessed by us

Users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to use any imported content.

⸻

## 7. Children’s Privacy

Because the App does not collect any personal information, it does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

⸻

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If changes are made, the “Last Updated” date will be revised accordingly.

⸻
